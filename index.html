About Me

A research profile built around food science, natural antioxidants, analytical chemistry, and translational applications in food systems.

I am a final-year PhD researcher in Food Technology at Université d’Artois, France. My doctoral work focuses on the valorisation of mangrove resources and their impact on food quality, particularly through the extraction and characterisation of bioactive compounds such as polyphenols. I investigate how these compounds interact with food matrices and improve oxidative stability and functional performance.


My work integrates chromatographic, spectroscopic, microbiological, and statistical approaches, with applications in oils, seafood, and cereal-based products. I am now seeking postdoctoral and research opportunities where I can contribute my expertise while continuing to grow through interdisciplinary collaboration.
