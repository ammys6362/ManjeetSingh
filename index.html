Ph.D. Candidate | Mangrove Bioactives | Food Quality | Functional Ingredients

I am a final-year Ph.D. researcher in Food Technology working on the valorisation of mangrove-derived resources and their impact on food quality. My research focuses on the extraction, physicochemical characterization, and functional evaluation of bioactive compounds, particularly polyphenols, to enhance oxidative stability and nutritional performance in food systems such as oils, seafood, and cereal-based products.

Using advanced chromatographic and spectroscopic techniques, I investigate antioxidant mechanisms and food matrix interactions. With international experience across Europe, Africa, and India, I contribute to interdisciplinary research at UMRT BioEcoAgro and I am now seeking postdoctoral opportunities within my field.
